About Me

I am an aspiring web developer based in Philadelphia. I'm originally from Doylestown, PA but lived in NYC for 10 years prior to moving to Philadelphia. I'm obsessed with all things art and design and love playing with modular synthesizers, reading sci-fi novels, collecting houseplants, and 35mm photography. I have a BFA in Film/Cinema Studies from Pratt Institute and an MFA in Digital & Interdisciplinary Practice from CUNY. I draw and do yoga every single day.
